about

Salisbury steak, pizza squares, chicken nuggets ... Across our local school district, hundreds of prepared school lunches go uneaten each week. Cafeteria was founded with the mission to eliminate school lunch waste. We collect uneaten meals from schools across the district and offer these preserved meals to our guests. Join us and experience the culinary delights of the school cafeteria. And don't worry about forgetting your milk money; milk is provided with every meal.
